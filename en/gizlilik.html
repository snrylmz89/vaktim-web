KVKK CLARIFICATION TEXT

Information Text on the Protection of Personal Data

Last Update Date: February 27, 2026

In accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (“KVKK”), as a sole proprietorship operating as a data controller, we inform you as follows regarding your personal data processed within the scope of the Vaktim application.

1. Data Controller

Data controller: Vaktim (Individual Business)
Communication: support@vaktim.app

2. Processed Personal Data

The following personal data may be processed within the scope of the application:

a) Identity and Contact Information

  • Email address
  • User name
  • avatar image

b) Usage and Activity Data

  • Prayer tracking data
  • Dhikr and prayer progress data
  • Quran reading statistics
  • Spiritual guide chat contents
  • Notification permission status
  • Premium subscription status

c) Device and Technical Data

  • Session information
  • App version
  • Authentication records

d) Location Data

Location information only:

  • Calculation of prayer times
  • Determination of Qibla direction

It is used on the device for Location data is not transferred to the server by default.

3. Special Personal Data

Prayer, prayer, dhikr and similar worship data processed within the scope of the application may, due to their nature, constitute indirect data regarding the person's religious belief.

In this context, the data in question can be considered as special personal data within the scope of Article 6 of the KVKK.

These data only:

  • With the user's explicit consent
  • For the purpose of providing the service
  • When cloud synchronization is activated upon user request

is being processed.

4. Purposes of Processing Personal Data

Your personal data;

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Storage of worship and progress data
  • Providing cloud synchronization with the user's explicit consent
  • Carrying out premium subscription transactions
  • Answering user support requests
  • Ensuring application security

is processed for its purposes.

APPLICATION;

  • Does not show ads
  • Does not do behavioral user tracking
  • Does not collect analytical data
  • Does not create cookie-based profiling

5. Legal Basis

Your personal data is processed based on the following legal reasons:

  • KVKK article 5/2-c: Being directly related to the establishment or execution of a contract
  • KVKK article 5/2-f: Legitimate interest of the data controller
  • KVKK article 5/1: Explicit consent (for cloud synchronization and sensitive data)
  • KVKK article 6/2: Processing of special data with explicit consent

6. Data Transfer

Your personal data may be shared with the following service providers:

Supabase (Germany – Frankfurt / European Union)

Account and synchronization data are stored within the borders of the European Union.

RevenueCat

Information regarding subscription status may be processed for premium subscription management purposes.

Your data:

  • Not sold to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Data is not transferred to brokers
  • Not shared for profiling purposes

The data is stored in European Union data centers and is not transferred outside the EU.

7. Data Security

Appropriate technical and administrative measures are taken to protect your personal data.

In this context:

  • Encrypted connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Authorized access control
  • Server security measures
  • Intrusion prevention mechanisms

is implemented.

However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Storage Period

Your personal data;

  • As long as the account is active
  • Within a reasonable time after the account deletion request
  • For the period required by legal obligations

is stored.

The user can delete his account and cloud data from within the app.

9. Your Rights Under KVKK

In accordance with Article 11 of KVKK;

  • Learning whether your personal data is being processed or not
  • Request information if processed
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Learning the third parties to whom transfers are made
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request data portability
  • Claiming compensation if you suffer damage

You have the rights.

Your applications support@vaktim.app
You can send it to the address.

You also have the right to file a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Authority.

10. Changes

This Information Text can be updated when deemed necessary.
The current version is published via the application or website.
