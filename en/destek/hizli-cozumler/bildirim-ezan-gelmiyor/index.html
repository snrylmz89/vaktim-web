Notification / Adhan Not Coming

Adhan notification problems are usually caused by system permission, focus mode or battery saver settings. Follow the steps below in order.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • In-app prayer notifications must be turned on.
  • Device notification permission and volume should be checked.
  • Battery saver/background restrictions should be removed.

Step by step

iOS

  1. Settings > Notifications > Vaktim: turn on all notifications.
  2. Add Vaktim to allowed apps in focus mode.
  3. Enable background app refresh.

Android

  1. Settings > Applications > Vaktim > Notifications: enable.
  2. Select Vaktim without restrictions in battery optimization.
  3. Control azan/time sound in notification channels.

Why Does It Happen?

  • Notification permission may not have been granted during the initial installation.
  • The device may be on silent or the media volume may be low.
  • Background restrictions may stop the application.

If It's Still Not Fixed

Send the time when you do not receive notifications, your device model and a screenshot to the support team.
