Quick Solution

Email Verification Link Not Working

Most verification errors occur due to expired tokens, incompletely copied links, or opening on a different device. Try the flow below in order.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Open the most recent verification email and click the link again.
  • Make sure you open the link fully in the browser (it should not be broken).
  • If unsuccessful, request a new verification email from the application.

Step by step

1) Use the Correct Email

  1. Open the last verification email sent, not old emails.
  2. Also check your spam and promotional folders.

2) Open the Link Fully

  1. If you are pasting the link into the browser, copy the entire link.
  2. Wait a few seconds when the verification page opens.

3) Generate New Link

  1. Use the “Resend verification email” option from the app.
  2. Try again with the new link.

Why Does It Happen?

  • The verification token may have expired.
  • The link may be missing or truncated.
  • A previously used verification link may have been tried again.

If It's Still Not Fixed

Include your account email, the error message you received, and the time you clicked on the link in your request.
