Qibla Direction Appears Wrong

Qibla direction deviations are mostly caused by compass sensor calibration and magnetic effect.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Keep location services on.
  • Calibrate the compass by drawing 8 on the phone.
  • Check again in an open area away from metal surfaces.

Step by step

  1. Enable location permission and precise location setting.
  2. Calibrate the phone by moving it horizontally and vertically several times.
  3. If there is a case or magnetic accessory, it can be removed temporarily.
  4. Open the compass again and check the qibla line.

Why Does It Happen?

  • Magnetic interference (table, vehicle, electronic devices).
  • Compass sensor calibration malfunction.
  • Incorrect city/location data.

If It's Still Not Fixed

Write to the support team with your device model, your city and a screenshot.
