Quran/Dhikr Progress Not Synced

Inconsistent progress data appearing on different devices is usually caused by internet connection, session or synchronization status.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Check that you're signed in with the same account on both devices.
  • Refresh your internet connection and reopen the application.
  • Verify that cloud sync setting is turned on.

Step by step

  1. Make sure your account information is the same.
  2. Turn sync on/off within the app.
  3. Go to the progress screen and refresh the data manually.
  4. Log out and log in again if necessary.

Why Does It Happen?

  • Using different accounts.
  • Disconnection or temporary server error.
  • Cloud sync permission is turned off.
