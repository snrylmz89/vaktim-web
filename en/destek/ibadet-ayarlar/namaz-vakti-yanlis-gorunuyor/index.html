Prayer Time Appears Wrong

Different time display is usually caused by wrong location, different calculation method or time zone.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Turn on location services and verify your city.
  • Update calculation method by region.
  • Set device time zone to automatic setting.

Step by step

  1. Compare the city information in the application with your current location.
  2. Choose the prayer time calculation method from Diyanet or your preferred method.
  3. Enable daylight saving / time zone automatic adjustment.
  4. Refresh the times by closing and reopening the application.

Why Does It Happen?

  • The old city record may have remained instead of GPS.
  • Times differ when the calculation method changes.
  • Manual clock use may cause deviation.

If It's Still Not Fixed

Add city name, displayed time, and expected time to your support request.
