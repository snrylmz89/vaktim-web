Opening First Notification / Adhan Settings

In order for adhan notifications to work regularly, operating system permissions as well as in-app reminder settings must be correct.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Activate notifications of relevant times within the application.
  • Turn on Vaktim notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Define permissions to Vaktim in phone silent/focus modes.

Step by step

In App

  1. Go to notification settings and select time-based adhan.
  2. Save your sound and vibration preferences.
  3. Send test notification and verify.

iOS / Android System Settings

  1. Notifications should be on for Vaktim.
  2. Check background refresh and battery throttle settings.
  3. Give exception to app in Focus mode / Do Not Disturb mode.

Why Does It Happen?

  • Notification permission may have been denied on initial setup.
  • Battery saver may be stopping the app in the background.
  • Focus mode may be suppressing the call to prayer sound.

If It's Still Not Fixed

Send a support request by writing down the time when you did not receive notifications and the device model.
