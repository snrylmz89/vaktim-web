What is my Time, How Do I Get Started?

My time; It is a spiritual companion application developed for prayer times, qibla, Quran, dhikr and daily worship routine.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Open the app, create an account or log in.
  • Give location permission and control prayer time method.
  • Turn on notification/adhan permissions and enable first reminders.

Step by step

1) Account Setup

  1. Sign up via email or log in with your existing account.
  2. Activate the account by confirming the verification email.
  3. Complete your profile name and basic preferences.

2) Basic Worship Settings

  1. Keep location permission on, verify city information.
  2. Adjust the prayer time calculation method according to your region.
  3. Personalize adhan sound and notification type.

Why is it important?

  • Incorrect initial settings cause time differences.
  • If notification permission is turned off, adhan reminders will not appear.
  • Cloud syncing may be limited on an unverified account.

If It's Still Not Fixed

When writing to the support team, provide your account email, device model and the step you are experiencing. This ensures a faster turnaround.
