How to Set Location and Prayer Time?

Location permission, time zone and calculation method should be checked together for correct prayer times. This guide offers the safest order for initial setup.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Keep location permission on “Always” or “While using the app”.
  • Verify city selection automatically or manually.
  • Choose the prayer time calculation method appropriate to your region.

Step by step

iOS

  1. Settings > Privacy and Security > Location Services > Vaktim.
  2. Turn on location and enable precise location.
  3. Check city and time zone within Vaktim.
  4. Update calculation method, save change.

Android

  1. Settings > Apps > Vaktim > Permissions.
  2. Enable location permission and exit battery optimization.
  3. Refresh your location within the app.
  4. Verify and recheck the prayer time method.

Why Does It Happen?

  • Location permission may be off or low sensitivity.
  • If the device's time zone is not automatic, the times may shift.
  • Choosing the wrong calculation method produces different results.

If It's Still Not Fixed

Include city name, screenshot, and device time zone information in your support request.
