Premium is Active but Features Are Not Opening

If the subscription is active but you see a locked feature, you need to complete the account pairing and purchase restoration steps.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Verify that you are logged in with the store account from which you purchased the subscription.
  • Run the “Restore Purchase” step from within the app.
  • Close and reopen the app and check your premium badge again.

Step by step

  1. Check your App Store / Google Play account.
  2. Make sure that your time account and store account belong to the same user.
  3. Start the restore process from the Premium screen.
  4. After the process, refresh the session and try the feature again.

Why Does It Happen?

  • You may have logged in with a different store account.
  • Account matching with payment confirmation may be delayed.
  • An internet outage may have interrupted the restore.

If It's Still Not Fixed

Add purchase receipt, account email, and device model to the request.
