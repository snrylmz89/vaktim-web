Password Reset and Login Problems

If you can't log in, first verify your account email, then complete the secure password reset flow. Wait for a short while to avoid account locking for repeated errors.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Verify that you are logged in with the correct email address.
  • Request a new connection with “Forgot My Password”.
  • Log in again after saving the new password.

Step by step

  1. Check your email address carefully on the login screen.
  2. Open the password reset link from the last email sent.
  3. Set a strong password and complete the process.
  4. Close and reopen the application and log in with the new password.

Why Does It Happen?

  • Use of old or incorrect email.
  • Expired reset link.
  • Temporary lock due to consecutive incorrect attempts.
