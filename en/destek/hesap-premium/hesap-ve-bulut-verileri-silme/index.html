How Do I Delete Account and Cloud Data?

Account deletion is permanent. Before completing the relevant steps, be sure to evaluate the possibility of active subscription and data return.

Last update: February 28, 2026

Solution in 30 Seconds

  • Go to your account settings in the app.
  • Confirm the “Delete My Cloud Data” option.
  • Complete the “Permanently Delete Account” step if necessary.

Step by step

  1. Open Profile/Settings.
  2. Start the cloud data cleansing process in the data management section.
  3. Complete the final confirmation screen to completely delete the account.
  4. After deletion, check that old data does not arrive when you log in with the same e-mail.

Why is it important?

  • Simply logging out without clearing cloud data will not delete data.
  • Account deletion cannot be undone.
  • Legal retention obligations may keep some records for a limited period of time.

If It's Still Not Fixed

When requesting data deletion, share your account email, transaction date and, if possible, a screen recording.
